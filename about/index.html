About

The Scandinavian PhD seminar provides an ideal possibility for PhD candidates from Scandinavian universities and research institutions to present ongoing, as well as, more finished research in Applied Economics in a supportive environment.

The aim is to open up a platform for young researchers to connect and engage with peers who share similarities in research topics, data access and foremost interest in the various subfields within Applied Economics.

The Scandinavian PhD seminar takes place via Zoom on Wednesday at 14:15 CEST. The set-up involves one-hour presentations with questions during the presentation. We are very much looking forward to meeting you in the seminar “room” and hearing about your exciting research projects.
